Ainzara film festival

funded by Gothe institute in 2020 is libya’s first competition for vertical videos (short films created and edited in aspect ratio (9:16) ) for tall-screen . It is an opportunity for young artists and filmmakers who are exploring the creative potential of the vertical format.

About the festival

Vertical films and videos are growing to be the new wave of films. Since nowadays cameras , phones and screens easily allow people to shoot and view all formats. Now’s the exact time to explore the beauty of tall screens.

About Ainzara

AinZara in east of Tripoli historical was the first place bombed from the sky in 1911 by italians and in 2020 was a war zone. It is also one of the largest places in tripoli and has a very crowded population.
